Top Custom Window Treatment Trends for 2024 in the Tri-State Area

As design trends evolve, window treatments continue to offer more than just light control. In the Tri-State area, look for solutions that emphasize energy efficiency, minimalistic aesthetics, and smart functionality in the coming year.

Energy-Efficient and Modern Solutions

Cellular Shades remain a top choice for their insulation benefits, while Roller and Solar Shades add a sleek, modern look that fits contemporary architecture. Solar Shades, in particular, help maintain natural views while filtering sunlight and UV rays. Their different openness factors (3-5%, low, or high) let you choose the balance between privacy, brightness, and visibility that best fits your style.

Motorization and Customization

In 2024, Tri-State area properties can benefit from these trends by embracing advanced materials, energy-efficient designs, and refined silhouettes that cater to modern lifestyles without compromising comfort and elegance.
